Welcome to MARV Earth! This guide explains the flow from joining the server to joining your first nation.

## 1. Joining the Server
Start Minecraft, go to Multiplayer, and add the server:

- **Server Address**: `marvgame.net`
- **Bedrock Port**: `19132`

## 2. Arrival at Spawn
When you first join, you'll arrive at the "Spawn Point" (HUB). Here you can access various linked servers by clicking on NPCs.

## 3. Go to the "sigen" Server!!
Click on the NPC labeled "sigen" in the HUB to gather resources.  
*Note: The resource server (sigen) is reset periodically! You cannot build your permanent home there.*

## 4. Go to the Earth Server with /neoearth!!
Type the command `/neoearth` in the chat to head to the Earth world!!

## 5. Use Discord to Join a Nation!!
Join our official Discord community to find a nation and join it!
- **Discord**: [Official Community](https://discord.gg/NfYyMnTfj3)
- **Discord**: [Nation & Town Promotion](https://discord.com/channels/1245921816959127673/1443877434561663058)

All users of MARVserver are deemed to have agreed to these terms.

## Chapter 1: General Rules
Users must respect others and public order, and cooperate with the orderly operation of the server. Users are obligated to follow the rulings of the administration.

## Chapter 2: Prohibited Actions
The following actions are strictly prohibited:

### 1. Restrictions on Speech and Expression
- Discrimination, slander, insults, or provocative remarks.
- Inappropriate or excessive sexual or violent expression.
- Leakage of personal information (regardless of consent).
- Instigating sensitive discussions regarding politics, religion, etc.

### 2. Nuisance to Other Users
- Indiscriminate mentions or direct messages.
- Bullying, hate speech, or excessive verbal abuse.

### 3. Fraud and Cheating
- **Use of Cheat Clients**: Xray, KillAura, AutoClicker, etc.
- **Unauthorized Textures**: Xray textures or those that display information not normally visible.
- **Exploiting Bugs**: Item duplication (dupe), wall hacking, or system abuse.
- **RMT (Real Money Trade)**: Exchanging in-game assets for real-world currency or value.

## Chapter 3: Server-Specific Regulations (NeoEarth)
- **PK (Player Kill)**: May be restricted depending on the situation.
- **Terrain Modification**: Large-scale terrain destruction or modification may be restricted.

## Chapter 4: Miscellaneous
- Use of multiple accounts is prohibited in principle (prior notification is required for family use, etc.).
- Fraudulent activities are prohibited.

For full details, please check the [Official Terms of Service](https://marvgame.com/en-US/terms-of-service).
